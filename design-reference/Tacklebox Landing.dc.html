FEATURE 01

Party Manager

Officers point /party start at a Google Sheet, and the roster lives there. Members sign up for weapons on the embed, officers paste assignments into the sheet, hit Sync, and Discord updates automatically — loadouts included.

  1. Click Sign Up, pick the weapon(s) you can play, and leave a note if you need one — low spec, early leave, whatever officers should know.
  2. Click Sign Up again any time to change your selection.
  3. Click Un-Sign Up to pull yourself from the sheet and the party entirely.
  4. Once officers sync, click View Loadout to see your assigned weapon and required gear.
  1. Run /party start <sheet URL> — share the sheet with the bot's service account as Editor first.
  2. Click Add Details to set the event title, UTC time, meeting place, and gear requirements.
  3. Copy signups from Column A into Column F next to their assigned weapon, then click Sync.
  4. Click End Party to close signups — parties auto-expire after 10 days, capped at 10 active per server.

FEATURE 02

Loot Split

Run /loot start after a fight and Tacklebox posts a message members react to join. Officers can add AFK players, exclude anyone who shouldn't be there, then hit Calculate — Tacklebox works out repairs, tax, and each player's cut, and drops a payout checklist your officers (or players, if you allow it) can check off.

  1. React with any emoji to join the split — one reaction counts, no matter which emoji.
  2. Excluded by an officer override? You won't appear on the payout checklist, even if you reacted.
  3. If self-marking is enabled, click Mark Myself Paid once you've been paid.
  1. Run /loot start — optionally set a voice channel for the Ping button, or override the guild's tax rate.
  2. Use Player Override to add AFK players or exclude anyone — up to 40 users per list.
  3. Click Calculate and enter Total Silver, Repair Cost, and Tax Rate (pre-filled with the guild default, 35% unless changed).
  4. Work the payout checklist — mark players paid, bank the unpaid, or allow self-marking. Buttons stop responding after 1 hour.
